Welcome

We at Gjøl Kro are all about the experience. We want you to enjoy the picturesque sunset over the fjord accompanied by the wide selection of traditional Danish dishes. We serve the classics with a modern twist, prepared from fresh, locally sourced ingredients. Come and feel the salty breeze of the sea while you enjoy a mouth-watering meal in the historic scenery.

It's not just food, it's an experience.

Restaurant where you can feel the cozy atmosphere while savour charming view and individual approach from the owners and staff.

The menu will vary depending on what raw goods are available during the season.

Guests can enjoy our à la carte menu in the restaurant, in the bar area and on the terrace.

The historical inn dates all the way back to 1851, when it had a thatch roof and served as the town’s gathering point. Unfortunately, it burned down around 1900 and was rebuild the year after, giving it its current form with stepped gables.
The inn is also where Hans Kirk wrote his novel ”The Fishermen” (Danish: Fiskerne), describing the conflict between the residents of Gjøl and a group of newcomer fishermen.
