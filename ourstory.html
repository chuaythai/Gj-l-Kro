YOUNG COUPLE
TAKES OVER INN

Hello! We are Peter & Nastazja and
we took over the inn's ownership in
April 2021, and have been living
there with our two beautiful children
ever since.

WE TAKE PRIDE IN OUR
HISTORY

The historical inn dates all the way back to 1851,
when it had a thatch roof and served as the
town’s gathering point. Unfortunately, it burned
down around 1900 and was rebuild the year after,
giving it its current form with stepped gables.

The inn is also where Hans Kirk wrote his novel ”The
Fishermen” (Danish: Fiskerne), describing the conflict between the
residents of Gjøl and a group of newcomer fishermen.

We want to emphasise our
creativity when it comes to giving a
modern twist to classic Danish
recipes.

Our menu vary based on the season
as we are taking advantage of local
and seasonal ingredients grown by
our local producers.
